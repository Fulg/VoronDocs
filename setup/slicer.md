# Slicer Setup

## Slicer Selection

There are two slicers that come with built in profiles for most Voron printers:

* Cura
* Super Slicer

Other slicers that can be configured to support Voron printers but may not have built in support:

* Prusa Slicer
* Simplify3D
* Slic3r
